The scenario of our game is in the following figure.

The game comprises three stages:

  • START At this stage, we prepare all required variables (for instance, the Number of attempts) and game screen, block movements of the Gun Sight. Go to the next level after pressing the SPACE button.
  • GAME Gameplay starts: listen to keyboard events and move the Gun Sight. After each pressing on the SPACE button, add the Bullet Hole on the Canvas and decrement the Number of attempts variable. When the Number of attempts reaches zero, we move to the next stage.
  • END Gameplay stops: block movements of the Gun Sight and creation of the new Bullet Hole. Put the final result on the screen.

Speaking of results, we need to measure the shooting accuracy. It is logical to assume that the closer you get to the center of the target, the higher the reward. Accordingly, a hit in the central circle score 10 points, and an outer circle gives 1 point. The other circles give in inverse proportion to their distance from the center. The current information is displayed on the Statusbar.

Objectives

There are 12 attempts. The requirements of constructing graphical objects remain the same as in previous tasks. For testing purposes, the initial position of the Gun Sight should be at the center of the Target. You should display the information about the game state in the Statusbar, as follows:

  • At the START, it contains text Press SPACE button to start the game.
  • During the GAME, it contains Shootings left: {1}, your score: {2}. Where {1} - number of remaining attempts, {2} - total score.
  • At the END, it contains Game over, your score: {1}. Where {1} - total score.
