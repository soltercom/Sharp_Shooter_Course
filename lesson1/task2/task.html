Description

It is time to add in our game the prime function there is the ability of the players to shoot at the target. The shooting consists of two parts: targeting and firing. The graphical object Gun Sight is responsible for aiming in our program. In our last task, we have learned how to draw this object. Now it is our job to make Gun Sight move under player control.

Let us select arrow buttons to control the movement of the Gun Sight. After each key pressed event, the sight moves to a new location. Once the guidance is complete, the user pushes a space button and makes a shot. As a result, the mark on the target Hole of Bullet appears. Meantime, the old bullet holes do not disappear from the screen.

Each time the position of the Gun Sight changes or a new Hole of Bullet appears, the Canvas will redraw as follows:

  1. Draw the Target on the Canvas as in the previous task.
  2. Draw the Bullet Holes on the Canvas.
  3. Draw the Gun Sight on the Canvas.

At the beginning of the program, you may create images for the Target, the Gun Sight, the Bullet Hole and place them in separate BufferedImage variables. Use those images with the method drawImage in the predefined method paintComponent of the Canvas.

Objectives

Implement components and display it on the Canvas:

The Target must satisfy the requirements of the previous task, that is:

  • The center point of the Target should be at (350, 350).
  • The Target should contain ten concentric circles with incrementing of radius in 30 pixels.
  • The colors of the circles should be Color.BLACK and Color.WHITE, and alternate.
  • The background color of the Target should be Color.DARK_GRAY.

For testing purposes, the initial position of the Gun Sight should be at the center of the Target. Draw your attention that the centers of those objects should coincide.
The following keys control the position of the Gun Sight:

  • VK_UP moves it to the up by 10 pixels,
  • VK_DOWN moves it to the down by 10 pixels,
  • VK_LEFT moves it to the left by 10 pixels,
  • VK_RIGHT moves it to the right by 10 pixels.
The Gun Sight must not leave the screen. Due to testing reasons, you should limit the horizontal and vertical coordinates of the center of the Gun Sight in the range from 40 to 660.

A new Bullet Hole should appear after each pressing on the SPACE button. It should be kept in mind that, as in the previous task, the color of bullets holes is different from Color.BLACK, Color.WHITE, Color.DARK_GRAY.
